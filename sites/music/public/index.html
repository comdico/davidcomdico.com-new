00:Album Notes

In photography a work print is a an initial print of a photograph used for evaluation and future editing. Sometimes work prints end up on museum walls as an original print of a great photographer is still a rare item.

This collection of tracks was recorded and mixed over a period of two years from many other tracks that were started and abandoned.

The completed songs here were used to understand various aspects of digital audio recording, songwriting, and musicianship.
