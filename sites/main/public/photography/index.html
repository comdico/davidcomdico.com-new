David Comdico is a photographer influenced by documentary traditions and contemporary visual culture and whose work explores the subtle tension between the ordinary and the extraordinary. With an eye for moments that might otherwise slip by unnoticed, David’s images often linger on the edges of urban life, street scenes, and fleeting human gestures.
